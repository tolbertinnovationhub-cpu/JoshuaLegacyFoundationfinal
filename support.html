FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Joshua Legacy Foundation is registered as a nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible in accordance with applicable tax laws. You'll receive a tax receipt upon donation.

How is my money used?

We maintain transparent financial practices. Approximately 85% of donations directly support programs, while 15% covers administration and operations. Detailed breakdowns available in our annual report.

Can I designate my donation to a specific program?

Yes! You can specify which program area (education, healthcare, leadership development, etc.) your donation should support. Contact us to arrange designated giving.

Is monthly giving really flexible?

Absolutely. There are no long-term commitments. You can increase, decrease, or cancel your monthly giving at any time with no penalties.

How can I sponsor a specific youth?

When you sponsor a youth ($1,200/year), you'll receive regular updates about their progress, receive their letters/photos, and can maintain contact throughout their development journey.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), bank transfers, PayPal, and other digital payment methods. Contact us for specific options available in your region.

Can I donate in-kind (goods/services)?

Yes! We welcome donations of educational materials, medical supplies, technology, professional services, and more. Contact us to discuss your specific donation.

How do I set up legacy giving?

You can include TJLF in your will, establish a charitable gift annuity, or transfer retirement accounts. Our team can guide you through the process. Contact us for legal guidance.
