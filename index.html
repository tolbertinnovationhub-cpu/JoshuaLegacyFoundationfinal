WELCOME TO JOSHUA LEGACY FOUNDATION

The Joshua Legacy Foundation (TJLF) is an innovative and transformative youth and leadership development organization committed to empowering at-risk, orphaned, underprivileged, and vulnerable children across Liberia. We envision a generation of empowered youth, rooted in divine purpose and guided by scriptural truth, who rise as leaders to restore, transform, and advance their nation.

THE CHALLENGE

Thousands of children in Liberia face poverty, orphanhood, and vulnerability. Many lack access to quality education, healthcare, and mentorship needed to become leaders. The Joshua Legacy Foundation addresses this by providing holistic interventions: spiritual grounding, cultural pride, and innovative education, transforming at-risk youth into empowered leaders capable of restoring their communities.

OUR APPROACH: STEMACH

We integrate Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, Arts, Culture, and Heritage into a holistic educational framework. This innovative system balances cutting-edge knowledge with ancestral wisdom, preparing youth for global competitiveness while honoring their cultural identity. Our programs span education, healthcare, leadership development, and community restoration across Liberia's 15 counties.
