OUR PROGRAMS

STEMACH Education & Holistic Development

Innovative educational frameworks designed to empower youth with 21st-century skills, cultural pride, and spiritual grounding.

The STEMACH Framework

We integrate Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, Arts, Culture, and Heritage into a holistic educational framework. This innovative system balances cutting-edge knowledge with ancestral wisdom, preparing youth for global competitiveness while honoring their cultural identity. Our programs span education, healthcare, leadership development, and community restoration across Liberia's 15 counties.

Program Structure

Multi-Tiered Approach

Foundation Level

Basic education and life skills for younger youth, establishing strong foundations in academics and personal development.

Intermediate Level

Advanced STEMACH education with specialized tracks and mentorship from professionals in various fields.

Leadership Level

Intensive training for emerging leaders who will serve as mentors and change agents in their communities.

Geographic Coverage

Our programs reach across all 15 counties of Liberia, with specialized initiatives for:

  • Montserrado County (Monrovia and surrounding areas)
  • Margibi County
  • Bong County
  • Nimba County
  • Grand Gedeh County
  • Lofa County
  • And all remaining counties...
