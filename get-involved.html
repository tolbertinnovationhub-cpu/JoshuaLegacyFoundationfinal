FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need experience to volunteer?

No! We welcome volunteers of all experience levels. If you have passion and willingness to serve, we can match you with appropriate roles and provide training as needed.

How much time commitment is required?

It depends on the role you choose. We have opportunities ranging from a few hours per month to full-time employment. Discuss your availability when you apply.

Can I get involved if I'm not in Liberia?

Yes! We have remote opportunities including virtual mentoring, consulting, fundraising, and advocacy work. Many of our international partners contribute from abroad.

Is there a cost to volunteer or participate?

No, there's no cost to get involved. Some roles may provide compensation or stipends depending on the position and your circumstances.

How long does the application process take?

Usually 1-2 weeks. We review your application, contact you for a brief conversation, and then match you with suitable opportunities.

Can organizations partner with TJLF?

Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with educational institutions, businesses, NGOs, and organizations. Contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities.

What if I want to try multiple roles?

You can! Many people start with volunteering and transition to internships or employment. You can also combine multiple roles based on your interests and availability.

How will I know about my impact?

We provide regular updates, impact reports, and stories about the youth and communities you've helped. You'll see the real difference your involvement makes.
