Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I hear back?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. During busy periods, it may take up to 3-5 business days.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We offer remote volunteer opportunities in areas like content creation, social media management, grant writing, and virtual mentoring. Contact us to learn more.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

Absolutely. We accept books, school supplies, technology equipment, and other resources. Contact us to discuss your donation and how it can help our programs.

How can I stay updated?

Subscribe to our newsletter by providing your email on the form above, follow our social media accounts, or check back to our website regularly for updates.

Is there a specific time to call?

We're available Monday through Friday, 9am to 5pm (Liberian Time). For urgent matters outside these hours, please send an email and we'll prioritize your inquiry.

Do you have a physical office I can visit?

Yes, we're based in Monrovia, Liberia. We welcome visitors by appointment. Please contact us ahead of time to schedule a visit.
