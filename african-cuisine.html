African Cuisines

Traditional Recipes

Jollof Rice

A popular dish in West Africa, especially Nigeria and Ghana, Jollof rice is made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a blend of spices.

Bunny Chow

A South African dish consisting of a hollowed-out loaf of bread filled with curry. This street food is both delicious and hearty.

Injera and Doro Wat

Traditional Ethiopian meal comprising injera (a sourdough flatbread) served with Doro Wat (spicy chicken stew).

Food Culture

African food culture is diverse and varies from region to region, influenced by climate, geography, and history. Meals are often communal and hold significant cultural importance.
