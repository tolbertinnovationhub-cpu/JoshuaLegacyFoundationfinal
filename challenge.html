The Situation in Liberia

Thousands of children in Liberia face poverty, orphanhood, and vulnerability. Many lack access to quality education, healthcare, and mentorship needed to become leaders. The Joshua Legacy Foundation addresses this critical challenge by providing holistic interventions: spiritual grounding, cultural pride, and innovative education, transforming at-risk youth into empowered leaders capable of restoring their communities.

Our Response

The Joshua Legacy Foundation doesn't just acknowledge these challenges; we actively address them through:

  • STEMACH Education: Integrating Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, Arts, Culture, and Heritage into a holistic framework
  • Spiritual Grounding: Rooting youth in faith and scriptural truth to guide their lives and decisions
  • Cultural Pride: Celebrating African heritage and helping youth reclaim their identity and purpose
  • Community Partnership: Building collaborative relationships with families, schools, and institutions
  • Comprehensive Support: Providing healthcare, mentorship, vocational training, and ongoing personal development
