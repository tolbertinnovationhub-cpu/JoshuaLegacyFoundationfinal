Our Growth Trajectory

Since inception, we've experienced significant growth in our reach and impact:

  • 2020: Foundation established, 200 youth reached in pilot programs
  • 2021: Expanded to 3 counties, 800 youth engaged
  • 2022: Growth to 8 counties, 2,000 youth participants
  • 2023: Reached 12 counties, 3,500 youth in programs
  • 2024: Expanded to 15 counties, 5,000+ youth served
  • 2025-2026: Target: Scale programs to serve 10,000+ youth annually across all 15 counties

Our Strategic Objectives

1. Holistic Youth Development

Provide comprehensive education combining STEMACH curriculum with spiritual formation, life skills, and cultural heritage programs. Ensure every youth receives mentorship and personalized development pathways toward their potential.

2. Leadership Pipeline

Identify, nurture, and deploy emerging leaders as mentors and community change agents. Build a network of youth leaders committed to their communities' restoration and national development.

3. Healthcare & Wellness

Provide preventive healthcare, health education, and wellness programs. Address malnutrition, disease prevention, and mental health support for vulnerable youth populations.

4. Economic Empowerment

Equip youth with vocational skills and entrepreneurial training. Create pathways to employment and sustainable income generation, breaking cycles of poverty.

5. Community Restoration

Transform communities through youth-led initiatives in infrastructure, education, healthcare, and cultural preservation. Strengthen family and community structures.

6. Cultural Pride & Identity

Reconnect youth to African heritage and Liberian culture. Develop cultural ambassadors who preserve and celebrate their traditions while engaging globally.

Expansion Goals

5-Year Strategic Plan

Achieve 100,000+ youth reached annually; establish regional leadership training centers; expand to Mano River Union countries
